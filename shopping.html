Shop in-store, at home or outdoors

Scan the QR code of a product on any surface in-store, on a PC or on a billboard. You will be forwarded to the Picksell app where you can pay for your item, order delivery or save it in the shopping cart to buy later. Shop wherever you are!

Gather all products in your single shopping cart

Put all your online and offline products in one shopping cart and buy, or save them until you ready to go.

Pay your favourite merchants safely and easily

Instead of entering your card into dozens of payment processors, use Picksell to pay for all your purchases from different stores.

Fully PSD2 compliant, meaning your payments go directly to the seller. No intermediaries mean less risk for your personal info.

We secure your data and privacy

Merchants get minimum information on customers only what's required to process an order, and never see card information or bank account info.

We request only the most essential info. You can refuse to provide the data, and still enjoy the highest level of service.

The user determines what data to share and how we use it.

We can easily limit access for a particular store or erase user info full stop.
